what is covid 19

COVID-19 is a contagious disease caused by a virus called SARS-CoV-2. It can make people feel sick in
different ways, from mild symptoms like a cold to more serious problems like difficulty breathing.

symptoms

Fever
Cough
Shortness of breath
Fatigue
Muscle aches
Headache
Loss of taste or smell
Sore throat
Congestion or runny nose
Nausea or vomiting
Diarrhea.
